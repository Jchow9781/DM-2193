This is REI, it's where I work!

Here are some fun facts about us!

  • REI stands for Recreational Equipment Incorporated
  • We sell outdoor equipment like tents, bikes, and skis
  • We were founded in 1938 by Lloyd and Mary Anderson
  • This location opened in late 2012 in the Puck Building in Soho Manhattan
  • We have over 150 stores throughout the US
  • The Soho store (pictured) is 1 of 5 flagship locations
  • The other 4 are in Seattle, Bloomington, Washington DC, and Denver
